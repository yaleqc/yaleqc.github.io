About

About the Yale Undergrad Quantum Computing (YuQC) group.

Nov 11, 2020

Purpose

We are an academic organization formed to promote and facilitate the participation of undergraduates in quantum information science at Yale.

Activites

Networking, Resources, Projects, Events.
(Depending on member interests.)

  • Develop open Source Quantum Computing Club Projects
  • Develop Tutorials
    • concepts: LinAlg, QM, Quantum Error Correction, etc
    • tools: Qiskit, Cirq, Pennylane, etc
  • Host Tours of Industry Quantum Computing Labs
  • Attend and possibly host QC Hackathons
  • Build an Alumni Network
  • Build Graduate Student Mentorship Program
  • Host research talks from academics, industry professionals, and club members
  • Host Literature Review Discussions
  • Facilitate Outreach to HS Students

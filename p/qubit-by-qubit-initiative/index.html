Qubit by Qubit Initiative

Check out this awesome outreach opportunity in quantum education.

Nov 11, 2020

Apply for paid and volunteer opportunities at the Qubit by Qubit Initiative.

Here are some of their current projects:

  • Developing homework, problem sets, or worksheets
  • Developing middle or high school curricula for quantum
  • Developing or editing content for textbook for high school students
  • Developing quantum workshop for K-12 students
  • Being a Teaching Assistant for intro course
  • Teaching quantum workshops to K-12 students

Please reach out to member Shoumik Chowdhury ‘21 to learn more!
